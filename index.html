About BowlerStats

BowlerStats is India's first cricket statistics website focused entirely on bowler performance during IPL 2025. Every match is broken down over-by-over with color-coded histograms: gold bars for normal overs, red bars for wicket overs, and green bars for dot overs. Tap or hover any bar to see the bowler name, exact runs conceded, and wickets taken. Each innings also includes expert commentary and a bowler summary table with economy rates.
